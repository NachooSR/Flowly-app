About the Project

Flowly is a personal finance tracking application designed to help users record their expenses and income in a simple and efficient way.

The project focuses on clarity and speed, allowing users to quickly log financial activity while still having the option to manage balances and savings in a more structured mode.

The goal of Flowly is to grow from a simple tracking tool into a more complete financial management platform.
