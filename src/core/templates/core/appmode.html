App Modes

Flowly offers different modes to help you manage your finances depending on your needs.

Tracking Mode

Designed for speed and simplicity. Quickly register your daily expenses and income without worrying about balances.

Perfect for users who want a fast way to track where their money goes. Every transaction is recorded so you can later analyze your spending habits and financial activity.

Balance Mode (In Development)

A more structured way to manage your finances. You start by defining your available balance and savings.

Every expense automatically subtracts from your balance, and every income increases it. This allows you to maintain a clear overview of your real financial situation.

Ideal for users who want stricter control of their money and long-term savings.
